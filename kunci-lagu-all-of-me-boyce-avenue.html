34. Someone has changed the name of this street from bay avenue to martin luther king way kalimat pasivenya adalah


The name of this street has been changed by someone from bay avenue to martin luther king way

Passive voice is focusing on the object and depending on the tense.

Person or someone who does the action is not necessary in this passive voice. Sometimes the word "by who " is omitted in the passive voice.

Let's take a look at your question given!

Pattern seems to be like this!

Subject (object of the active sentence ) +has/have + been + Verb 3 ( past participle).

Someone has changed the name of this street from bay avenue to Martin Luther king ( Active )

Note : the name of this street ( object )

It should be written like this!

First alternative answer : The name of this street has been changed from bay avenue to Martin Luther king.

Second alternative answer : The name of this street has been changed by someone from bay avenue to Martin Luther king.


Material : English (Passive voice )
