After Rapunzel turns to be12 years old, the witch shuts her away in a one-room high tower in the middle of the forest with no door, stair or door and only one window.

Setting of Rapunzel: the story setting is in Deep in a Forest.Characters: Rapunzel Parents, Witch and Princess.Conflicts: The conflict start when rapunzel father steal rampon from witch's garden, and after Rapunzel grow up, A prince from a kingdom  fall in love with Rapunzel , but the witch make the price fall down from tower and axile Rapunzel to a desert.Story Ending: After Rapunzel exiled to a desert, she meet with the price and have a happy ending and become Queen and King.Moral Value: When our life have destined to happen then no one, no matter how much power we have, cannot stop destiny from happening.        

Penjelasan:

Fairy Tale atau Dongeng atau Cerita Rakyat (Folk Tale) merupakan cerita yang berasal dari suatu daerah yang biasanya berdasarkan dari hal-hal yang bersifat supranatural, dan mistik.

Dongeng biasanya difahami sebagai cerita yang tidak benar-benar akan terjadi di dunia nyata.

Unsur- unsur dalam dongeng, terdapat 2 unsur, yakni Unsur intrinsik yang membentuk cerita dongeng berasal dari dalam, unsur intrinsik dongeng antara lain tema, amanat, latar, sudut pandang, tokoh, penokohan, alur.

Unsur ekstrinsik merupakan unsur yang membentuk cerita dongeng dari luar dan unsur ekstrinsik meliputi latar belakang masyarakat, latar belakang pengarang.

28. the first page of a story book is numbered from page 1 onwards. all the pages in storybook are numbered. to print all the page numbers of storybook, 65 digits needed. how many pages are there in the storybook?​


Jawab:

There are 37 pages in the storybook.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

The sequence of one-digit page numbers constructs a 9 digit number like this: 123456789.And then, it comes to two-digit page numbers. Each one of these needs 2 digit. So, from 10 to n (n is a two-digit number), there will be 2(n-9)-digit number.

Therefore, the addition of both rules will result in the total digits needed to print all the page numbers in sequence, starting from number 1.

    9 + 2(n-9) = 65

⇔ 2(n-9) = 65 - 9

⇔ 2(n-9) = 56

⇔ n-9 = 56/2

⇔ n-9 = 28

⇔ n = 28+9

⇔ n = 37

Conclusion: There are 37 pages in the storybook.

Proof:

From 1 to 9: 9 digits needed.From 10 to 19: 20 additional digits needed, so the total is 29 digits.From 20 to 29: 20 additional digits needed, so the total is 49 digits.From 30 to 37: 16 digits additional needed, so the total is 65 digits.

39. Contoh soal narative tentang legend of the first banana story


Once upon a time, there was a beautiful girl called Maria. She was tall, with black eyes, and long shining hair. The colour of her skin was a clear light brow.
One morning, while she was collecting firewood, she met a young man.
He looked like a hunter. He was tall, handsome and very neatly dressed. No one knew
who he was or where he came from. Nor did anyone know his name. Maria liked him. He liked her too.
Maria did not know this but he was an anito or a spirit from the sky.
They became friends. Although they were good friends for a long time, the young man never asked Maria to became his wife.
This made Maria very unhappy. “I have no parents or brothers or sisters,” she said. “You too are alone. I am sure we could be happy together.”
“I didn’t want to tell you this,” the young man said, “but I am an anito. I cannot marry a human being. I have to return to the sky.”
Maria was surprised. She did not know what to say. She held his hands tightly.
“please let me go,” said the young man. “I had hoped you would never find out who I am.”
There was a blinding flash of light and the young man disappeared. But as Maria was holding on to his hands so tightly, he left them behind.
Of course, Maria was frightened. She ran home and buried them in her garden. Soon she saw a strange plant growing where she had buried them. The plant grew fast.
Then some fruit appeared. It was yellow and shaped like a man’s hand with fingers on it. It was the first banana.
