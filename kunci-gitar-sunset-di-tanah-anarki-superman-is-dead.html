16. Tolong dong buat deskripsi tentang jrx superman is dead Sama artinya ya...


Superman is Dead (sometimes referred to simply as S.I.D) is a punk rock band hailing from Bali, formed in Kuta. The band has 3 members, namely Bobby Kool (lead vocal, guitar), Eka Rock (bass and backing vocal), Jerinx (drummer).

The Name Superman Is Dead

The name 'Superman is Dead' started its evolution from Stone Temple Pilot's "Superman Silvergun". It was changed to "Superman is Dead" because the band agreed that nobody is perfect.

SID formed in 1995, drawn by their common interests of Green Day and NOFX. Their influences soon extended to the punk 'n roll genre a la Supersuckers, Living End and Social Distortion.

Warped Tour 2009
June – July 2009, Superman Is Dead held an American tour, playing 16 concerts in 16 cities. 11 of these shows were on the ‘Vans Warped Tour’ and the last 5 gigs were titled ‘From Bali With Rock’.

Artinya :

Superman is Dead (kadang-kadang disebut hanya sebagai S.I.D) adalah band punk rock yang berasal dari Bali, terbentuk di Kuta. Band ini memiliki 3 anggota, yaitu Bobby Kool (vokalis, gitar), Eka Rock (bass dan backing vocal), Jerinx (drummer).


Nama Superman Is Dead Nama 'Superman is Dead' memulai evolusinya dari Stone Temple Pilot "Superman Silvergun". Itu diubah menjadi "Superman is Dead" karena band tersebut sepakat bahwa tidak ada yang sempurna.

SID dibentuk pada tahun 1995, digambar oleh kepentingan bersama Green Day dan NOFX. Pengaruh mereka segera berlanjut ke genre punk 'n roll ala Supersuckers, End End dan Social Distortion.


Warped Tour 2009
Juni - Juli 2009, Superman Is Dead menggelar tur Amerika, menggelar 16 konser di 16 kota. 11 dari pertunjukan ini ada di 'Vans Warped Tour' dan 5 pertunjukan terakhir berjudul 'Dari Bali With Rock'.
