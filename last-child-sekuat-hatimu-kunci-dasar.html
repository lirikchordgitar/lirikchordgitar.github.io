5. Last year, this child _____. *A.were adoptedB.will be adoptedC.was adoptedD.is adoptedJwb dngan tepat terimakasi​


Jawab: C. Was adopted

Last year, this child _____. *

A. were adopted (Passive-Past-Plural)

B. will be adopted (Passive-Future)

C. was adopted (Passive-Past-Singular)

D. is adopted (Passive-Present-Singular)

Pembahasan:

✅ Karena ada Last Year, maka sudah pasti Past Tense.

✅ Subject (this child) singular

✅ maka Berdasarkan pilihan ganda, yg paling tepat adalah C. Was adopted

Jadi:

Adverb of time, S + was/were + V3

Last Year, this child was adopted

==== Semoga Membantu ====

Mapel: Bahasa Inggris

Kelas: SMP

Materi: Passive Voice

Kode Mapel: 5
