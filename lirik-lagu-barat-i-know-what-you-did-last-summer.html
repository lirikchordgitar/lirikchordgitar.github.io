Jawaban: A. Saw (Salah) D. See (Benar)

Penjelasan: Yang dikatakan komen benar, maaf sudah menyesatkan

Jawaban :

d. see

Penjelasan :

(+) Subject – Verb 2 – Time Signals.

(-) Subject did not – Verb 1 – Time Signals.

(?) Did – Subject – Verb 1 – Time Signals?

Example :

(+) He visited a movie studio yesterday.

(-) He did not visit a movie studio yesterday.

(?) Did he visit a movie studio yesterday?
